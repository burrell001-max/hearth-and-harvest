About Hearth & Harvest

Hi, I'm Kent Williams — the person behind Hearth & Harvest. This site started as a simple collection of the recipes I actually cook at home: the pasta dishes I make on a busy weeknight, the breakfast I turn to on a slow Sunday morning, and the desserts I bring when I want to impress without spending all day in the kitchen.

I'm not a professional chef. I'm a home cook who got tired of recipe sites that bury a simple dish under ten paragraphs of unrelated stories before you ever reach the ingredients list. Every recipe here is written the way I'd want to read it myself: clear ingredients, straightforward steps, and no filler.

What you'll find here

Hearth & Harvest is organized around real life — quick weeknight dinners, healthy options, breakfasts, and the occasional indulgent dessert. Every recipe has been tested in my own kitchen before it makes it onto the site. If something doesn't work, it doesn't get published.

Why "Hearth & Harvest"

A hearth is where a home is warmed and fed. A harvest is what makes good cooking possible in the first place — good ingredients, simply prepared. That's the whole philosophy of this site: nothing fancier than it needs to be, but made with care.

Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you find something worth cooking.

— Kent Williams
