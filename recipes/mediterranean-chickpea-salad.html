Vibrant Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Prep Time:
15 mins
Cook Time:
0 mins
Servings:
4
Calories:
290 kcal

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 large English cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the Base: In a large mixing bowl, combine the rinsed chickpeas, diced cucumber, and halved cherry tomatoes.
  2. Whisk the Vinaigrette: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, fresh lemon juice, minced garlic, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Toss and Garnish: Pour the vinaigrette over the salad base and toss gently to combine. Top with crumbled feta cheese and fresh parsley before serving.
