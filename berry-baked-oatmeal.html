Mixed Berry Baked Oatmeal

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 35 mins Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 2 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup pure maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups milk (any variety)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen mixed berries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and lightly grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the rolled oats, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, maple syrup, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  4. Scatter half of the mixed berries along the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Cover evenly with the dry oats mixture, then pour the wet ingredients over the oats.
  5. Scatter the remaining berries over the top. Bake for 35 minutes until the top is beautifully golden brown and the oatmeal is completely set. Serve warm!
