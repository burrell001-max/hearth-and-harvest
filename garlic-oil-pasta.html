Garlic & Oil Pasta

Dinner • 15 Minutes • Easy

An elegant, classic Italian staple made with robust olive oil, perfectly toasted garlic, and a hint of red pepper flakes. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner that feels premium.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti or your favorite long pasta
  • 4 tbsp High-quality extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp Red pepper flakes (adjust for heat)
  • Handful Fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, add olive oil and sliced garlic to a cold skillet. Turn heat to medium-low to slowly infuse the oil.
  3. Cook garlic until it becomes fragrant and turns a light golden brown (do not let it burn!). Stir in the red pepper flakes.
  4. Tongs the cooked pasta directly from the pot into the skillet, bringing along a splash of starchy pasta water.
  5. Toss vigorously over low heat to create a glossy sauce. Stir in fresh parsley and serve immediately.
