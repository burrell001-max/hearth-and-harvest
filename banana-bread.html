Classic Moist Banana Bread

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 60 mins Servings: 8 slices

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, thoroughly mashed
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips or walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and butter a standard 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until perfectly smooth. Stir the melted butter completely into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until fully liquid.
  4. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt across the mixture, then fold the flour in gently until just combined (do not overmix). Fold in chocolate chips or nuts if using.
  5. Pour the smooth batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 50 to 60 minutes until a toothpick inserted right into the center emerges completely clean. Let cool before slicing.
