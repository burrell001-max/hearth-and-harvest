Vegetable Curry

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup spinach
  • Salt, to taste
  • Steamed rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion and cook for 4-5 minutes until softened.
  2. Stir in garlic, ginger, and curry powder, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, stirring to combine.
  4. Add the potatoes and cauliflower. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for 15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in the chickpeas and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Fold in the spinach until wilted, season with salt to taste, and serve hot over steamed rice.
