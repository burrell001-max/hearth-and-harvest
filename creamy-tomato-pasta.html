Creamy Tomato Pasta

Dinner • 20 Minutes • Medium

Rich, velvety tomato sauce simmered with fresh herbs and a luxurious touch of heavy cream poured over perfectly al dente noodles. A comforting classic elevated for restaurant quality at home.

Ingredients

  • 200g Penne or Rigatoni pasta
  • 1 can (400g) Crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Heavy cream
  • Handful Fresh basil leaves
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water according to the package directions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until soft and fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, season with a pinch of salt and pepper, and bring to a gentle simmer for 10 minutes to deepen flavors.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in the heavy cream. Let it incorporate until the sauce turns a beautiful rich orange hue.
  5. Drain pasta and toss it directly into the sauce. Garnish generously with torn fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
