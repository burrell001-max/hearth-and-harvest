Greek Salad

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 0 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 English cucumber, sliced into half-moons
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 7 oz block feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine the tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, green pepper, and olives in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently to combine.
  4. Transfer to a serving platter and place the block of feta on top.
  5. Drizzle a little extra olive oil and a sprinkle of oregano over the feta and serve immediately.
