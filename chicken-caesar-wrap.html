Chicken Caesar Wrap

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/3 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup shaved parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup croutons, roughly crushed

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until cooked through.
  2. Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into thin strips.
  3. In a bowl, toss the chopped romaine with the Caesar dressing until well coated.
  4. Lay a tortilla flat and layer with the dressed romaine, sliced chicken, shaved parmesan, and crushed croutons.
  5. Fold in the sides and roll tightly into a wrap. Slice in half and serve immediately.
