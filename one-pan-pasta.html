One-Pan Pasta Delicacy

Quick Meals • 12 Minutes • Super Easy

Effortless cooking meets premium flavor. By cooking the pasta directly in the vegetable broth and tomato juices, the noodles absorb maximum taste profile while releasing starches that create an instantly rich, self-thickening sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g Linguine or Spaghetti
  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small Onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups Vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Fresh baby spinach leaves

Instructions

  1. In a large straight-sided skillet or wide pan, lay down the dry pasta flat.
  2. Arrange the halved cherry tomatoes, sliced onion, and garlic around the noodles inside the same vessel.
  3. Pour in the liquid broth and olive oil. Bring the entire pan to a full boil over high heat.
  4. Using tongs, stir and turn the pasta frequently to keep it from sticking together. Cook for roughly 9 minutes until the liquid reduces to a thick sauce and the pasta is al dente.
  5. Toss in the baby spinach leaves during the final minute of cooking until wilted. Season with salt, fresh cracked pepper, and serve immediately.
