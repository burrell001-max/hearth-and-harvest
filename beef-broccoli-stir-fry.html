Easy Beef & Broccoli Stir-Fry

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 lb flank steak, thinly sliced across the grain
  • 2 heads of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil, divided
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water to make the stir-fry sauce.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat. Add broccoli florets and cook for 3 minutes until bright green and crisp-tender. Remove broccoli from skillet.
  3. Add remaining tablespoon of oil to the same skillet. Add the sliced beef in a flat layer and cook without moving for 2 minutes to get a nice sear, then toss and cook for 1 more minute.
  4. Pour the prepared sauce into the skillet with the beef. Bring it to a boil; it will thicken up quickly within a minute.
  5. Toss the broccoli back into the skillet to coat everything beautifully in the glossy brown sauce. Top with sesame seeds and serve over white rice.
