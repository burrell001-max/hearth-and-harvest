Loaded Avocado Toast

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 thick slices of artisanal sourdough bread
  • 1 large ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Everything Bagel seasoning
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered
  • Drizzle of extra virgin olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the sourdough bread slices until golden brown and beautifully crisp.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado flesh together with the lemon juice and a tiny pinch of salt using a fork.
  3. Spread the seasoned avocado smash evenly across the warm toasted sourdough.
  4. Top with the quartered cherry tomatoes, scatter Everything Bagel seasoning generously, and finish with a light drizzle of high-quality olive oil.
